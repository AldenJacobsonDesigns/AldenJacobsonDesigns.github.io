Alden Jacobson Engineering Design and Drafting

Alden Jacobson Engineering Design and Drafting Inc. (AJ D&D) was founded in April of 2016 to provide design engineering drawings to clients throughout Ontario. This company is comprised of many years of Civil, Structural, and Mechanical Engineering experience. AJ D&D provides the following services:

  • Residential projects

  • Commercial projects

  • Municipal projects

Certification

AJ D&D is comprised of one licensed Professional Engineer with experience in Civil, Structural, and Mechanical Engineering design. It holds a Certificate of Authorization from Professional Engineers Ontario. AJ D&D is committed to providing efficient and reliable service to all clients, ensuring that all drawings follow up-to-date building codes.
