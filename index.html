About us

Alden Jacobson Engineering Design and Drafting Inc. (AJ D&D) was founded in April of 2016 to provide design engineering drawings to clients throughout Ontario. This company is comprised of one licensed Professional Engineer with extensive experience in Civil, Structural, and Mechanical Engineering design. AJ D&D provides the following services:

  • Site inspections

  • Stamped detailed drawings

  • Stamped letters to attain construction permits

For the following project types:

  • Residential projects

  • Commercial projects

  • Municipal projects

Certification

Member of Professional Engineers Ontario (PEO).

Mission

AJ D&D is committed to providing efficient and reliable service to all clients, ensuring that all drawings follow up-to-date building codes.
